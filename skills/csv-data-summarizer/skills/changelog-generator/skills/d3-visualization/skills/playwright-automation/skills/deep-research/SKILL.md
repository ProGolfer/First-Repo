# Deep Research

Executes autonomous multi-step research for comprehensive analysis.

## When to Use

- Market analysis and research
- Competitive landscaping
- Literature reviews
- Technology research
- Due diligence
- Any topic requiring comprehensive research

## Instructions

1. Clarify research objectives and scope with user
2. Formulate research questions
3. Execute multi-step research:
   - Search for relevant sources
   - Extract key information
   - Synthesize findings
   - Identify gaps and follow-up questions
   - Iterate as needed
4. Compile comprehensive report with:
   - Executive summary
   - Key findings
   - Supporting evidence
   - Sources and citations
   - Recommendations

## Output Format

- Research objectives
- Methodology
- Key findings (organized by theme)
- Data and evidence
- Sources list
- Conclusions and recommendations
